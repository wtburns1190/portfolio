Experience

Several years of graphic and web design experience with over a decade in customer service

Customer Support

About Me

Frequently asked questions, get knowledge before hand

I want to further my career as a graphic designer and web developer. I left a career in development support at a Fortune-50 company to pursue a passion. I have completed my A.A.S. in Graphic Design, and added another A.A.S. in Web Design along the way. With roles in graphic design and print production for a small print shop, graphic and web design for a large scale retail import vendor, as well as several smaller freelance projects, I am ready to take the next step in my career.

Together, we can do it. I have years of experience in team environments as well as success as a freelance artist working directly with clients. I know that I can adapt myself to any role and love getting to know new people. Input, intellect, adaptability, and strategic thinking are all strengths I possess. Others say I am funny, smart, well-spoken, and fun to be around. I enjoy working with others. I believe working together, everyone involved can achieve more.

Art, music, and nature. From classical to contemporary, nouveau to deco, dada to bauhaus, impressionism to realism. From jazz to pop, hip hop to rock, country to indie, R&B to soul.
